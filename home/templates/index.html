Welcome to Hotel Green Mint

Your Comfort, Our Commitment

Hotel Green Mint was established by our illustrious founder, Mr. Arvind Singh, Chairmen of UP Group of Industry, with the aim of serving foods and accommodations to people. Hotel Green Mint has presence in different parts of Pan India. All our hotels have luxurious rooms, gyms, swimming pools, and indoor sports facilities. We serve all types of foods, such as Indian and Continental, cooked by well-trained chefs. You are doing various sightseeing near our hotels to enjoy nature. We have created our hotel to be unique and full of individual expressions of modern travel and contemporary living. Whatever you want, whenever you want it, the Hotel Green Mint has something to fulfill your aspirations.
