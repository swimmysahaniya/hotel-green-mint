Rules and Cancellation Policies

HOTEL GREEN MINT Management will highly appreciate your collaboration in abiding by these rules and regulations, whose aim is to ensure peaceful and safe stay for our Guests.

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 2:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out will be considered, subject to availability.

Extra person charges - Rs 1000 per person.

GST/Taxes will be levied additionally and applicable as per government directives.

100% advance payment must be submitted at the time of check-in.

MODE OF PAYMENT (CASH, DEBIT CARD ,CREDIT CARD)

We do not accept personal cheques for payment.

It is mandatory for all customers and their visiting guests to share their ID cards. Foreign nationals are required to present their passport and valid visa.

In case the guest fails to appear in the hotel by 6 p.m. of the accommodation day despite making a reservation, the fee for the room shall be charged by the hotel.

We do not allow visitors to enter the guest room.

Accommodation will be provided only to those guests whose details are registered at the hotel reception.

Guests will be provided room keys upon arrival and only after completing registration at the hotel reception desk.

Guests are required to show a photo ID at the time of check-in. Please note that all special requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

A no refundable policy is a set of terms and conditions stating that a business will not accept returns, exchanges or refunds for products or services purchased under any circumstances.
